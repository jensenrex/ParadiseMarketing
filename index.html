Our Team

Our management team are made up of Jensen Chow and Absolom Aube.

Jensen holds law and accounting degrees from University of Technology, Sydney. His family has called PNG home since the 1890s. Absolom went to university in Philippines and manages a team of web developers serving American clients.

Our Heritage

Jensen’s father, Joseph Chow,OBE, while a Catholic priest, set up schools and medical centers and was a high school teacher in Milne Bay for 7 years. Joseph also served on PNG’s Tourism Promotion Authority. Jensen’s late uncle, Sir Henry Chow, was the Mayor of Rabul before independence and served as Chairman of the National Research Institute. Jensen’s first cousins, Adrian Chow and Fabian Chow, presently sit as the chairmen of PNG NRL and Fresh Produce Development Agency.
